NATIONAL MUSEUM

  • trpws2012
  • July 2, 2020
  • Leave a Comment

Aim – To give an idea to the children about the richness and uniqueness of our culture and tradition. To let them know about India and it’s unique diversity.

Museum is a building in which objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and exhibited. One can learn many things in a visit to a museum as it preserves our ancient history and defines it in a pictorial way which expresses many things in one sight.

They gained knowledge about the significance of the objects in respect of history, culture and artistic excellence and achievements. It was a step to get past to present and appreciate our ancient custom and culture. Children got to learn the significance of our rites and rituals and various customs that are deep buried and never spoken of in any history book.

Looking back at history, children saw where we have come from and how society has progressed, surely some of them also had visions and ideas in their mind which one day could their work on this museum’s wall itself.
